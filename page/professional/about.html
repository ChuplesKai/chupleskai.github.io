Introduction

I am a robotics engineer and software developer, programming for over 12 years. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work on extensive, multi-year software projects, including contributions to open-source software such as the PRACSYS simulation and planning framework and the Open Motion Planning Library (OMPL).

Currently, I am the Lead Technical Architect at Airlitix Inc., working on autonomous greenhouse monitoring using drones. More information about this recent work be found on my Projects page. My experience is primarily task- and motion-planning for manipulator robots, which I studied for the majority of my academic career. See the Academia page for more details on my research and publications.
